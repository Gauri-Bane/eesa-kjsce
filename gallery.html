Abhiyantriki

EESA had organized two events ‘follow the flare’ and ‘Circuit frenzy’ for the annual technical fest of KJSCE. It was held on 5th and 6th October. Qualification rounds were on 5th October and on 6th October final rounds were held. Events were concluded by the prize distribution.

Banglore IV 2019

The EESA Council organized an Industrial Visit to ISRO Bangalore and JVS Electronics Pvt. Limited from January 20-23, 2019. A total of 48 students from different engineering branches along with two faculty members from Dept. of Electronics Engineering: Prof. S. A. Hanumante and Prof. Lekha Das accompanied for the Industrial Visit.

Hyderabad IV 2020

The start of the year 2020 witnessed a bunch of students getting insight into the internal working of “ETDC” and “Coca-Cola” companies located at Hyderabad. Historic sites including various other attractions made the High tech city visit worth it.A total of 68 students from various departments of the college along with three faculties of the electronics department, Dr. Sudha Gupta, Prof. Nilesh Lakade and Dr. Ninad Mehandale joined the trip.

Social Work

For the very first time, EESA Council stepped in to conduct a social activity that is, a step to enrich the unprivileged children with general knowledge and a career guidance session. Five members of the Council namely Meet Mehta, Rinkal Keniya, Hardik Patel, Gauri Bane, and Siddharth Vaidya along with the faculty members Prof. Makarand Kulkarni and Prof. Bharti Khedkar reached the venue - ' Shelter Don Bosco' at 10:30 AM on 21st of February 2019.

Teachers' Day Celebration

EESA Council celebrated Teachers’ Day and Engineers’ day on 17th September wherein all the faculties as well as non-teaching staff of the electronics department were invited. The event which began at 9:30 a.m. included speech by HOD Sir, fun games like Dumsheradas, Antakshari, etc. The celebration ended with a cake cutting ceremony and distribution of snacks at 10:30 a.m.

Workshops

The EESA council organizes various workshops for all the students right from th FY to the TY. These workshops are very interactive and precise. Some of the workshops conducted by EESA include Interactive Web Programming, Arduino, BEE , Node MCU, Circuit Designing, etc.

Calculator Workshop

The EESA council kick-starts their work by organizing the calculator workshop in the month of August. 100-120 students attended the workshop from all department. The workshop is usually a hour long. Technical team of EESA council makes the attendees familiar with the scientific calculator.

Lecture Series

EESA Council had organized a series of four lectures on various topics which helped in different aspects of their life such as Time/Stress Management, Career Counseling, etc. The first lecture was held on 3rd October- Stress and Time Management by Mrs. Rajashri M Paralikhar. The second was led by Mr. Mukesh Jain on Career Counselling with future perspectives(4th October). Mr. Puneet Thakur led the third lecture on GRE and Overseas Education(9th October). The last lecture from the series was held on 9th November by Mr. Gajendra Deshmukh on CAT/GMAT/GATE.
