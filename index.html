What is EESA


Electronics Engineering Students Association (EESA) is a student's organization at K.J Somaiya College of Engineering, Vidyavihar. In the department of Electronics, a wide variety of opportunities subsist for students to be concerned and we focus on enhancing these ideas. Many of our activities are focused on developing an interest in this vast domain of electronics while hosting several events with local professionals as well the undergraduates who are keen on sharing their knowledge. These sessions lead the potential to grasp concepts practically or employment possibilities in the future. In addition to the technical activities, engaging in social activities for the welfare of our society is at the outset since last year. Our blog aims at providing information about the unknown technologies behind a device, the latest inventions or excerpts from professional techies interviews, etc. As we head deeper in this era of online media, our success in publishing annual technical magazine "Periscope" leaves a significant mark every year.

Gaining skills, broadening our knowledge, Team Management, Leadership Qualities are some of the most rewarding aspects of being involved in this organization
