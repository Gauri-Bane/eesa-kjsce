When AI meets IoT - AIoT

Artificial Intelligence systems expect to be smarter only one-tenth as compared to humans by 2035. The internet-connected smart cars and self-driving cars are speeding up the development through IoT.

So, what’s next? When these independent technologies come together and provide lethal benefits to the growing industries. The Artificial Intelligence of Things commonly called as AIoT is the mixture of AI and IoT to gain more efficient IoT advancements, improve human-machine intercommunication and enhance other related data policies in the market. Artificial Intelligence is the use of machine intelligence to assume information, Internet of Things, on the other hand, is the interconnected combination of several sensors which gathers user data.

Artificial Intelligence can enhance the overall outcome of major tools or method out there, if not all, provided then it is used in the accurate context. Internet of things which is still believed to be a relatively new technology can make rapid progress by using the advantages of machine learning and AI. IoT enabled devices will act as sensors which are related to a cloud computing program. The purpose of the device will be to obtain all the accessible data as per the form and send it back to the cloud for computation. The task of preparing the data and storing information will be restricted to a cloud computing program in this kind of atmosphere.

An example can be explained as an AI-enabled smart speaker can treat the trigger speech using a Natural Language Processing (NLP) pattern preferably than transmitting all the sound capture to the cloud for computing. AI-enabled IoT system will be more strong, more reliable with sufficient quantity of data to do prediction so that the algorithms can run efficiently in a local context.

AIoT is about changing the IoT information into valuable data for an enhanced decision-making method, with processing executed in a position where it is most required. The age of the smart city will allow ever-smarter end devices to not only be data generators but info aggregators through the AIoT decision-making scholars. At present there are millions of smart gadgets and the application of Artificial Intelligence to construct secure and robust solutions to the upcoming 5G world will be a necessity rather than a choice.


Is AIoT the future?

The idea of AIoT is still comparatively fresh and while the hype is expanding, the important idea is to distribute between what is currently feasible and what is yet a move off. Is AIoT ‘the next big idea?’

Artificial Intelligence of Things will change almost every industry including automotive, aeronautics, banking, healthcare, production and stock series. We have invested our trust in daily life devices, we store our plans, contacts and images on our phones, provide smart devices to control the lights and heating in our homes and the list continues. We trust these devices with the most important assets which are our data. In the past chips soon became outdated, however on-device technology is at a position that the chips being utilised can adapt to upgrades and progress in AI technology and settle to the AI models of the future.

Cars communicating to lampposts, traffic lights and street symbols may seem an insane notion now though, in the future, this will become a real phenomenon.

Yes, the next generation is witnessing AIoT revolution.

~Pooja Nambiar
